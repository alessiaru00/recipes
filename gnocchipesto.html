Gnocchi with Basil Pesto

Baked gnocchi with basil and Parmesan cheese.

Experience Gnocchi with Basil Pesto enveloped in a green sauce of fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, and Parmesan.
This simple yet sublime dish captures the essence of Italian comfort, offering a burst of flavors in every forkful.

Features

  • Author: Some guy in Genova
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Preparation: 30 min
  • Doses: 4 people
  • Average cost: €€

Ingredients

  • Gnocchi: 500g
  • Basil: 150g
  • Pine Nuts: 50g
  • Parmesan Cheese: 100g
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • EVO Oil: 80g
  • Salt: to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the potato gnocchi according to the package instructions or until they float
    to the surface. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine fresh basil leaves, grated Parmesan cheese, toasted pine nuts, and peeled garlic cloves.
    Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  3. With the food processor running, gradually pour in the extra-virgin olive oil in a steady stream. Continue processing until
    the pesto reaches a smooth consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large bowl, toss the cooked and drained gnocchi with the freshly made pesto sauce. Ensure the gnocchi are well-coated
    with the pesto.
  5. Place slices of fresh mozzarella on top of the gnocchi. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the dish.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden.
  7. Transfer the gnocchi with pesto to serving plates. Optionally, you can drizzle a bit of extra olive oil over the top.
  8. Garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese and a few whole pine nuts toasted, and enjoy!
